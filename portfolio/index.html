Current cohort

There are 12 startups on the first Ignite 2.0 programme. They are:

Happity – A platform connecting parents and pre-school activity providers (a ~£2B market in the UK).

Langu – A platform connecting language students with professional language teachers for live, one-to-one lessons in a virtual classroom.

Yooz – A money remittance service that supports low-value payments between the UK and India.

Prolifiko – A digital coach for writing, powered by persuasive technology and AI. Think Fitbit or Strava for writing.

DebtCase – Debt collection for the UK SME market, which is owed ~£0.5T.

CGHero – Connecting companies to a global community of approved industry CG artists to meet the demands of challenging creative projects.

Shoesie – Fashion customisation & ecommerce software that allows footwear brands to offer customisation, or NIKEiD for anyone.

Our Daily Thread – An editorial platform that democratically curates the latest and greatest fashion drops, daily.

theWebShed – Helping digital agencies improve how they manage and deliver the SEO campaigns they run for their clients.

Guardian Angel – Simplifying the bereavement process; connecting families with funeral directors and digitising the funeral directors’ offline processes.

Currikula – A new way to improve essays and assignments; from reference management to bespoke feedback on students’ writing.

Link Big – The world’s first “link engine optimisation”, helping increase AOV/ROI from mobile assets, tailored and aligned to brands' and publishers' business goals.
